 Prime Electrical Services – This is Company Website

Welcome to the official website repository for **Prime Electrical Services**, a professional electrical services company committed to delivering safe, reliable, and high-quality electrical solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial clients.


 About Prime Electrical Services

Prime Electrical Services specializes in:
- Electrical installation
- Maintenance and repairs
- Power systems and wiring
- Lighting solutions
- Electrical safety inspections

The website serves as an online presence to showcase services, provide company information, and allow customers to easily get in touch.

 Website Features

-  **Index Page** – Overview of the company and its values  
-  **Services Page** – Detailed description of electrical services offered  
-  **About page** – Company background, mission, and expertise  
-  **Contact Page** – Contact form, phone number, and location  
-  **Media page** – shows all company videos and audio
-  **service request page** - shows the form to request the services you want
  
 Technologies Used

- **HTML** – Structure of the website  
- **CSS** – Styling and layout
